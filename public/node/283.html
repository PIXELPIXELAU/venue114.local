Located off David Low Way, next to Jack Morgan Park and only a short stroll to Coolum Beach, the Coolum Civic Centre is a large multi-functional facility that lends itself to a range of activities and events, from theatre productions and weddings to dance and fitness classes.

The hall is supported by a stage, complete with a large backstage area, including change rooms and a loading dock.

The kitchen is fully equipped with domestic appliances, along with a large selection of crockery and cutlery. Adjacent to the foyer, the kitchen has a serving counter.

In addition to the hall, there is a meeting room suitable for up to 12 people and a small community office for up to 3 people.

There is limited parking on-site for up to approximately 30 vehicles and 2 designated accessible parking spaces.

 

The Booking Officer for this venue is located at Venue 114, 114 Sportsmans Parade, Bokarina.

For enquiries, including availability please contact the Booking Officer, 5413 1416 or email communityspaces@sunshinecoast.qld.gov.au

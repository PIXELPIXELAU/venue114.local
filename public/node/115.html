Accessibility

Venue 114 has a range of accessible features to support the inclusion of all community members including the following:

  • Wheelchair car parks
  • Accessible toilets on each level
  • Elevators
  • Flat floor venue
  • Designated wheel chair areas during theatre seated events, please refer to the specific event page or contact Box Office for more information


If you require further assistance when visiting Venue 114 please contact our Box Office and discuss with our friendly staff. 

Venue 114 is supported by the Queensland Government’s Companion Card Program, to learn more please contact our Box Office, 07 5413 1400.

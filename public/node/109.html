Venue 114’s Commercial Kitchen is located on the ground floor in close proximity to the halls with servery points to the Foyer and Large Courtyard. This space is available for hire in conjunction with an event; not available for exclusive hire. This space includes stainless steel benches, dishwasher, glass washer, stoves, commercial ovens, deep fryer, cold room, refrigerators and small freezer. Refer to the Planning tools for more information on inclusions.

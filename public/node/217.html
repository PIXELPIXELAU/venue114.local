What's Online | For Kids

Last modified at Wed 22 Apr 2020

It's no secret that Venue 114 loves creating incredible experiences and bringing people together, though we may not be able to do that right now we can still stay connected and bring you amazing at home experiences and entertainment.

Kindy and Pre-School

 

The Very Hungry Caterpillar

Bring story time alive with sock puppets and other creative props.

 

 

 

 

At Home Wiggles Concert

Start your very own Wiggles concert with the help of YouTube.

 

 

 

Owl and Pussycat TV

Come join the adventurous duo and their adorable friends as they take you on fun-packed journeys from the garden and night sky to the art studio.

Little Critters Club

Get creative, crafty and musical with the Little Critters Team, where we love sharing music, stories and art. Tune in anytime and look out for new exciting videos, downloads and activities each and every week.

Primary and Secondary

 

AudioPlay

Create epic adventures that put kids at the heart of the story with highly active imaginative play - free until 30 April.

 

 

 

 

Digital Detectives

Use your super sleuthing skills to complete a series of challenges each week and become an expert user of Sunshine Coast Libraries eLibrary.

 

 

 

 

 

Queensland Ballet Online Dance Classes 

Dance can offer physical, social and creative benefits of all ages. Enjoy free online community classes.

 

 

 

 

Shake and Stir Online Drama Classes

Enjoy weekly drama classes with interactive play and acting designed to build invaluable life skills.

 

 

 

 

 

Let's Friday! with The Listies

Kid's favourites The Listies are back with a weekly live-stream of the kind of madness that only they can make. Tune in to Facebook and LOL out.

Sea Life Sunshine Coast Virtual Aquarium

Join Sea Life Sunshine Coast for live streams of our keepers, aquarists and our star sea creatures. Get exclusive activity sheets, craft activities and downloadables!

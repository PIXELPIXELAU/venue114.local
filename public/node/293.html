Magic Men

For the first time on the Sunshine Coast, Australia’s most anticipated male dancers are heating up the town with Magic Men’s “ALL STAR TOUR” only at Venue 114.

Showcasing the most talented entertainers from each capital city, this heart-stopping live performance, featuring Will Parfitt, is a one-of-a-kind stage show that tears up the stage with a high-energy atmosphere, smooth moves and a lighting spectacle guaranteed to make it a night out to remember.

Celebrating diversity, grab your partner, your friends or your work colleagues for the hot entertainment ticket of 2022! 

Voted Australia’s BEST MALE DANCE GROUP, meet the magic and find out what makes this group of performers so special.

The beautiful Lake View Room is a large function space on level two ideal for private celebrations and corporate functions. This versatile space includes a projector and screen, views of Lake Kawana, parquetry timber flooring, a private balcony and amenities and a kitchenette with bar servery and kitchen equipment.

Big Day In

Join us on Thursday 17 March for the 2022 BiG Day In Sunshine Coast IT Careers Conference for high school students. 

Students from Year 9-12 will hear from local, national & multi national organisations to learn about where a career in ICT/technology can take them.  Participating organisations include Microsoft, Technology One, Defence Force Recruiting, Entag, Services Australia, Woolworths, Sunshine Coast Council & loads more to come.  

*No charge for accompanying parents/teachers

Built in 2005, Venue 114 is a three-level modern facility with 15 different spaces for hire including three halls, an outdoor stage and two indoor stages equipped with high quality sound and lighting infrastructure and lakeside courtyard. Owned and operated by Sunshine Coast Council, this in-demand venue hosts a variety of events, festivals, conferences, expos, community group classes, private functions, children’s entertainment and live music performances.

Covid-19 Safety Measures

Sunshine Coast Councils owned and operated venues will enforce social distancing restrictions in accordance with the Queensland Government's step-down approach to eased restrictions and has developed Pandemic Protocols, outlining the guidelines for visiting and using our spaces under COVID-19 restrictions.

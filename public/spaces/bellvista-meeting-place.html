Space

The Bellvista Meeting Place is a self-service venue located in the Bellvista residential area, nestled centrally within the vibrant Canavan Gracie Recreational Park and overlooks the Bellvista Lake. The venue is in close proximity to the park, public tennis court, children’s playground and shops.

  • Open plan floor space
  • Adjoining timber deck with large shade sail
  • Kitchenette
  • Toilet amenities including disabled toilets and additional public amenities available outside the venue
  • Air conditioning
This Space is Suitable for

Community Groups | Meetings | Small Seminars | Training Rooms | Workshops | Private Functions | Low Physical Activities i.e. yoga or pilates

Equipment Inclusions:
  • 16 rectangular tables
  • 70 chairs
  • Large mobile whiteboard
  • 370L fridge with freezer plus additional separate 140 Ltr fridge
  • Microwave
  • Dishwasher
  • 20L Urn
  • Crockery and glassware

*It is the responsibility of the hirer to set up and pack down the venue

Fees and Charges

Venue hire and bond charges are dependent on the hirer and event categories. Please refer to the fees and charges in the above quick links for more information. Cleaning and security charges may also apply and depend on the event circumstances, please discuss with the Venue Officer.

Accessibility and Parking:
  • Wheelchair accessible
  • 14 designated car parks onsite including disabled spaces with additional off-street parking available

 

The Booking Officer for this venue is located at Venue 114, 114 Sportsmans Parade, Bokarina.

For enquiries, including availability please contact the Booking Officer, 5413 1416 or email communityspaces@sunshinecoast.qld.gov.au

Sunshine Coast Councils owned and operated venues will enforce social distancing restrictions in accordance with the Queensland Government's step-down approach to eased restrictions. For further information regarding venue capacities and safety measurements, please refer to the Community Spaces Pandemic Protocols. 

Space

Meeting Room B is located waterside of this self-service venue with scenic views of Lake Parrearra. This space is ideal for community groups, meetings, small seminars, training rooms or low physical activities like Yoga or Pilates and private functions.

Kawana Island Meeting Place offers two interchangeable spaces with a removable wall that allows the rooms to be combined for one larger space if required. Located within the Kawana Island residential areas alongside Double Bay Beach Park and the waters of Lake Parrearra, the venue is in close proximity to eateries and a convenience store.

  • Two meeting rooms which can be booked individually or together as one larger space
  • Adjoining kitchen shared between the two spaces
  • Toilet amenities including disabled toilets shared between the two spaces
This Space is Suitable for

Community Groups | Meetings | Small Seminars | Training Rooms | Workshops | Private Functions | Low Physical Activities i.e. yoga or pilates

Equipment Inclusions
  • 5 rectangular tables per meeting room
  • 30 chairs per meeting room
  • Large mobile whiteboard per meeting room
  • Domestic fridge with freezer
  • Oven
  • Stove top
  • 8L urn
  • Kettle
  • Microwave
  • Crockery and cutlery

*It is the responsibility of the hirer to set up and pack down the venue

Fees and Charges

Venue hire and bond charges are dependent on the hirer and event categories. Please refer to the fees and charges in the above quick links for more information. Cleaning and security charges may also apply and depend on the event circumstances, please discuss with the Venue Officer.

Accessibility and Parking
  • Wheelchair accessible
  • 5 car parks including disabled space with additional street parking is along Grand Parade

 

The Booking Officer for this venue is located at Venue 114, 114 Sportsmans Parade, Bokarina.

For enquiries, including availability, please contact the Booking Officer, 5413 1400 or email communityspaces@sunshinecoast.qld.gov.au

ZEST

Zest by nature and Zest by attitude, ZEST is the must-stop-coffee-spot overlooking the stunning lake at Lake Kawana. Located in the courtyard of Venue 114 and serving hugs in mugs, Zest thrives on the ethos of helping you to feel good. Enjoy freshly made barista coffee, complimented with delicious, locally sourced feel good treats, served to you with earth-loving sustainable practices.

We also throw in free WIFI!

Open Monday-Friday 8am-12pm for the general public and extended hours during various events.

Start your day with a little Zest!

Venue 114 Temporarily Closed

Last modified at Wed 18 Mar 2020

Following the latest coronavirus (COVID-19) announcement from the Prime Minster advising against non-essential gatherings of more than 100 people, Venue 114 has decided to close to the public from midnight tonight (Wednesday 18 March) until further notice. This closure includes the five Community Spaces Venue 114 manages; Beerwah Community Hall, Bellvista Meeting Place, Kawana Community Hall, Kawana Island Meeting Place and Maroochy Arts and Ecology Centre.

We apologise for the inconvenience caused but please understand Council’s priority at this time is to protect and support our community, visitors, staff, volunteers and artists from infection. We hope to work together as a community to make this process as smooth as possible.

During our time of closure to the public, Venue 114 staff will be available to take future bookings via phone and email: 07 5413 1400 or venue114@sunshinecoast.qld.gov.au . Please note as Venue 114 is closed until further notice, Venue 114 is not acquiring event bookings that require ticketing at this stage. Staff will also make contact with all individual ticket holders for events at Venue 114 that were due to take place to arrange a full refund.

The management of the novel coronavirus (COVID-19) is an evolving situation, we ask that you be prepared for the fact that what is in place today may change tomorrow as the pandemic evolves.

Council is committed to supporting Queensland Health as the lead agency responsible for responding to novel coronavirus (COVID-19) and will ensure our community is kept informed. For more information visit sunshinecoast.qld.gov.au

Anyone with health concerns can call 13 HEALTH (13 43 25 84) or find up-to-date reliable information on the Queensland Health website.

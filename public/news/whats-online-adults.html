What's Online | For Adults

Last modified at Wed 22 Apr 2020

It's no secret that Venue 114 loves creating incredible experiences and bringing people together, though we may not be able to do that right now we can still stay connected and bring you amazing at home experiences and entertainment.

Online Concerts and Digital Events

The IsoLate Late Show

Lock down and stay-up late with an online live concert to raise money for Australia's creative workforce.

Every Friday, 8pm

 

One World #TogetherAtHome Album

Stream the global broadcast and digital special, supporting frontline healthcare workers and the WHO, World Health Organisation.

 

 

Dementia Australia - Care for the Carer

This online event explores strategies, resources and support services for maintaining positive carer wellbeing.

Wednesday 13 May, 9.30am -11.30am

 

Dementia Australia - Grief, Loss and Dementia

An overview of dementia and caring for someone who has dementia.

Tuesday 16 June, 10am - 2pm

 

 

 

Meet the Maker at Home

Weekly experiences delivering local produce to your door and followed with an online session featuring some of best local brewers, farmers and chefs bringing a mix of live interactive online sessions including information and tips.
